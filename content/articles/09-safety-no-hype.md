Safety is mostly about avoiding extremes. If you take multiple products, overlaps can increase your intake of certain ingredients without you noticing. Keeping a short list of what you take is a simple protection.

Be cautious around marketing that frames supplements as urgent solutions. That framing encourages impulse buying and overuse. A calmer approach is to follow label guidance and discuss any changes with a clinician if you have health conditions or take medication.
